About

I am a PhD Candidate in International Relations at Florida International University. My research focuses on global governance, international organizations, rising powers, space governance, human rights, democratization, climate change, and international relations theory.

My dissertation examines the roles of China and India at the United Nations, with particular attention to how rising powers shape multilateral institutions from within.
